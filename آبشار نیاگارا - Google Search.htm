    Waterfall in North America
    Niagara Falls is the collective name for three waterfalls that straddle the international border between the Canadian province Ontario and the American state of New York. They form the southern end of the Niagara Gorge. Wikipedia
    Address: Niagara Falls, NY 14303, United States
    Elevation: 99 m
    Height: 51 m
    Flow rate: 2,400 m³/s
    Bridges: Rainbow Bridge, Whirlpool Rapids Bridge
    Did you know: It is believed that with the rate of erosion, Niagara Falls will be gone in 50,000 years. softschools.com
